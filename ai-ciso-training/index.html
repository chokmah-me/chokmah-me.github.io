Direct Response to Common CISO Compliance Objectives

1. Core Compliance Goals
  1. Collect every standard pushed by regulators (DoE, CISA, etc.)
  2. Collect each customer’s private cyber clauses
  3. Cross-correlate to maximize overlap (minimum investment, maximum coverage)
  4. Turn the resulting “golden” control set into formal product requirements
  5. Keep the mapping alive as standards and contracts evolve
